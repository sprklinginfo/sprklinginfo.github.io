How will we get there?

OK it is time to think about how we get there? Frankly speaking,it will actually largely depend on what I will get from Library Systems/Services Assessment. Now I will try to outline several ideas that could potentially be on my real work plan to support one or more strategic goals.
First is to enhance web scale discovery. Are all our IR records or local collections included in Summon? Are there information silos? Evaluate our search results, like how relevant? Too many search results? what to show and what to suppress will be tough decision. Do we need include Open Access content like in Digital Commons? This will need work with other staff and faculty memebers. Can we use "Automated Contextual Research Assistance" through localized recommendations, research guides, and live reference help to improve UX? Do we need have social networking tools, like reviews, rating and sharing like LibraryThing or BiblioCommons?

Enhance Web Scale Discovery

  • Harvest/add records from IR or local collections
  • How relevant are search results?
  • Include Open Access content to Summon?
  • Automated Contextual Research Assistance
  • Extend to have social networking tools (reviews, rating, and reading list?)
